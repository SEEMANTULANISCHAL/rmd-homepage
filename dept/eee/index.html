ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT
The department has an intake of 60 students and it offers subjects relevant to current industrial needs like Power Electronics, Electrical Machines, Control & Instrumentation, Microprocessors and Microcontrollers, Solid State Drives, High Voltage Engineering, Power System Engineering etc. The department has an engineering design and analytical software tools such as MATLAB / SIMULINK with necessary tool boxes, PSCAD and A.U. Power Lab for Power System Simulation Lab etc., A well-equipped conference hall with LCD projector for guest lectures by eminent technologists is available. The students are taken industrial visits and they undergo in-plant training in reputed organizations. Apart from the Central Library, the department is provided with a separate library comprising of 498 volumes of books, journals and computer based tutorials in the following areas.

  • Digital Signal Processing

  • Microprocessor and its applications

  • Renewable Energy Sources

  • Artificial Intelligence and Expert System

  • Electrical Drives and Control

The department is maintaining well equipped laboratory. The requirement and standards exactly matches with the University curriculum.

  • Electrical Machines Laboratory

  • Power System Simulation Laboratory

  • Control Systems Laboratory

  • Measurements and Instrumentation Laboratory

  • Circuits and Devices Laboratory

  • Renewable Energy Systems Laboratory

The department has a close networking relationship with professional bodies and experts from leading institution to impart their knowledge to our department students in the various emerging fields of electrical engineering. Well equipped seminar hall of 140.93 Sq.mts is provided to our department to import knowledge from eminent persons shining in relevant areas.

The department is accredited by NBA (National Board of Accreditation) from the academic year 2019-20 to 2022-23
