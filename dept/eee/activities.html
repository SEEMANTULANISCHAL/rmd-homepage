ACTIVITIES
The twelve faculty members are encouraged to pursue their research programme and participate in various seminars and workshops to improve their skills in recent trends in the field of Electrical and Electronics Engineering. They also attend faculty development programmes conducted by other colleges to enhance their teaching skills.

Every year, a National Conference is being organized in the month of April in which research scholars and students from different colleges take part.

Intra department Mini Project contest for 2nd year students and 3rd year students is being conducted from the academic year 2015-2016.

The students present papers in Symposia organized by various engineering colleges. The department offers a stimulating environment for its students to do their project work and to identify & seek solutions to the problems of industries.
